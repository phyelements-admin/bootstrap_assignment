COMPANY OVERVIEW 

    Dialight is the world leader in LED industrial lighting technology with millions of LED fixtures installed worldwide.
    Our story began in 1938 in Brooklyn, NY, where we produced instrument panel lights for aircraft. In 1971, just one 
    year after the introduction of the LED, we launched our first LED product. Since then, we have revolutionized the use
    of LEDs and ONLY LEDs, to provide superior lighting for traffic control, indicators,structural towers and industrial 
    work sites around the world. Learn more about our products and people in our company video.
